
# Business Continuity Plan Outline Example

This example illustrates a high-level **Business Continuity Plan (BCP)** ensuring continuity of essential business functions during major disruptions.

---

## Critical Services

- Online Banking Platform
- Customer Support Call Center
- Payment Processing Systems

---

## RTO and RPO

| Process | RTO | RPO |
|---------|------|-----|
| Online Banking | 2 hours | 15 minutes |
| Call Center | 4 hours | 1 hour |
| Payroll Processing | 24 hours | 12 hours |

---

## Continuity Strategies

- Failover to cloud hosting providers.
- Load-balanced web services.
- Work-from-home contingency operations.

---

## Communication Plan

| Group | Channel | Frequency |
|--------|---------|------------|
| Employees | SMS, Email | Every 4 hours |
| Customers | Website banner, Email | Daily updates |
| Regulators | Formal Notification | As required |

---

## Testing Schedule

- Tabletop exercise: Quarterly
- Simulation test: Semi-annually
- Full interruption test: Annually

---

---

## Exam Relevance

- Validates understanding of **BCP structure**.
- Demonstrates integration with **RTO/RPO targets**.
- Shows importance of stakeholder communications.
