# Risk & Asset Classification Example

**Purpose:** Define how information and systems are classified for protection and risk assessment.

| Asset | Classification | Reason |
|-------|---------------|--------|
| Customer Data | Confidential | Legal & privacy requirements |
| Financial Records | Restricted | High financial impact if compromised |
| Public Website | Public | No confidentiality concerns |
| HR Records | Secret | Sensitive personal employee data |

**Notes:** Classification impacts controls, monitoring, and incident response.
