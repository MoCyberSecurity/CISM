# Security Metrics & KPIs — Domain 1

This file provides practical examples of metrics and KPIs to monitor information security governance effectiveness.

## 1. Strategic Alignment
- **Metric:** Percentage of security initiatives aligned with business objectives.
- **KPI Example:** ≥ 90% of projects reviewed by the governance committee are mapped to business goals.

## 2. Risk Management
- **Metric:** Number of identified risks mitigated within defined risk appetite.
- **KPI Example:** 95% of critical risks have mitigation plans executed within 3 months.

## 3. Value Delivery
- **Metric:** Cost-effectiveness of security investments.
- **KPI Example:** Security spend as % of IT budget remains within ±5% of planned value.

## 4. Resource Optimization
- **Metric:** Staff training coverage and completion rate.
- **KPI Example:** 100% of employees complete annual security awareness training.

## 5. Performance Measurement
- **Metric:** Average time to detect and respond to incidents.
- **KPI Example:** Mean Time to Detect (MTTD) ≤ 4 hours; Mean Time to Respond (MTTR) ≤ 24 hours.

## 6. Assurance & Compliance
- **Metric:** Percentage of successful audits.
- **KPI Example:** ≥ 95% of internal and external audits have no major non-compliance findings.

## Notes
- Metrics should be measurable, time-bound, and aligned with organisational objectives.
- Metrics drive continuous improvement and support executive reporting.
